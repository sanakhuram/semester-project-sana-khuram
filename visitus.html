Food and drinks

Join us for a delightful dining experience at our museum's eateries, perfect for kids and adults alike. Open Monday through Sunday , from 9 AM to 6 PM, our cafes offer a variety of tasty treats to fuel your exploration. Discover freshly brewed coffee, wholesome sandwiches, and vibrant salads made with locally sourced ingredients. Indulge in scrumptious pastries and sweet treats that are sure to delight young and old alike. Our friendly staff is here to ensure your visit is enjoyable, whether you're grabbing a quick bite between exhibits or settling in for a leisurely lunch. Join us and refuel your curiosity as you savor the flavors of discovery at our museum's dining spots.

Shop

Step into our museum's shop, where the magic of discovery never ends. From Monday to Friday, we welcome you from 9 AM to 6 PM , ensuring ample time for you to explore our treasure trove of memorabilia, gifts, and activity packs. Whether you're seeking a memento of your museum adventure or searching for the perfect gift, our doors are open, ready to inspire and delight. Join us on Saturday and Sundays, from 10 AM to 4 PM, and extend the wonder of science long after you've left our halls. Explore, discover, and experience the joy of learning with us, every day of the week.
